Our Services

CarSafeKids exclusively install child restraints in the Eastern suburbs of Melbourne, 6 days a week. As a mobile service, we can conveniently come to you, or you can drop by our workshop in Blackburn.

We will educate you in the correct use of your product as all installations receive full operational instructions.

We are fully up to date with current state and federal laws and requirements as well as new product development. This ensures correct installation and advice.

With CarSafeKids youwill receive a certificate of compliance as well as the peace of mind you have had your seats installed by an Australian Child Restraints Resource Initiative (ACRI) accredited fitter.
